Quick Action Tips

Power Outage

Hover for Tip

Keep freezers and refrigerators closed. An unopened fridge will keep food cold for about 4 hours.

Fire Safety

Hover for Tip

Stay low to the ground. If your clothes catch fire: Stop, Drop, and Roll immediately.

Flooding

Hover for Tip

Turn Around, Don't Drown! Just 6 inches of moving water can knock you down.

First Aid

Hover for Tip

Apply direct pressure to any bleeding wound using clean cloth or gauze until help arrives.
